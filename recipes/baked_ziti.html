Baked Ziti

Description

This baked ziti recipe features al dente pasta combined with a savory marinara meat sauce made with Italian sausage, and is loaded with mozzarella and pecorino romano cheese.

This comforting and hearty dish is perfect for a big family dinner or can be easily prepared as a freezer meal.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz of Dried ziti pasta
  • 1 tsbp of Olive oil
  • 1 lb of Ground italian sausage
  • 4 cloves of Garlic
  • 32 oz of Marinara sauce (Rao's brand is preferred)
  • 1/2 tsbp of Red pepper flakes
  • 3 cups of Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Minced fresh parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit and butter a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the ziti pasta about a minute less than the package directions suggest for al dente. Then, drain the pasta and set it aside, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large deep skillet over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the ground Italian sausage and cook, breaking it up into crumbles, for about 5-6 minutes until it's cooked through. Drain any excess grease.
  4. Return the cooked sausage to the skillet and add the minced garlic. Stir well to combine.
  5. Pour in the marinara sauce and add the red pepper flakes. Stir everything together and then lower the heat to keep the sauce at a simmer for about 10 minutes, until heated through and slightly thickened.
  6. Remove the skillet from the heat and stir in the cooked pasta until it is well combined with the sauce.
  7. Transfer half of the ziti mixture into the prepared baking dish. Top this layer with half of the shredded mozzarella cheese and half of the Pecorino Romano cheese.
  8. Spread the remaining ziti mixture over the cheese layer in the baking dish. Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella and Pecorino Romano cheeses evenly over the top.
  9. Bake the ziti in the preheated oven, uncovered, for 20 minutes, or until it is hot and bubbly. Let it cool for 5-10 minutes before garnishing with minced fresh parsley and serving.
